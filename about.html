About This Experiment

Thank you for your interest in our study, where we examine how clinicians interact with AI-driven medical recommendations.

In this experiment, we use an AI Clinical Decision Support (CDS) platform—powered by a Large Language Model (LLM)—that helps clinicians make better decisions by offering draft recommendations.

In this experiment, you will engage in answering a demographic questionnaire, analyzing some clinical scenarios and diagnoses provided by AI (which you can trust or not trust), and providing feedback on the AI if you do not trust it. You will also fill out a post-experiment survey. Your answers will be saved on the server once you finish the experiment.

Your participation will help us understand how clinicians evaluate and trust AI-generated medical predictions. We appreciate your time and insights, as they will contribute to improving the design and reliability of AI Clinical Decision Support systems for healthcare professionals.
